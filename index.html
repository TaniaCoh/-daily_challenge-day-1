What I am reading now

  • The Stormlight Archive - Brandon Sanderson.
    The Stormlight Archive is the 2nd volume of a 10 books saga. On the planet Roshar, a world of stones swept by storms, a violent conflict rages for several years between ten armed forces against one enemy. As the storms get stronger and stronger, a mysterious past slowly resurfaces, with the orders of the Radiant Knights who deserted millennia earlier. Only their relics remain, swords and armors conferring superhuman abilities, and for which the nations are ready to tear each other apart.
  • The Thorn Birds - Colleen McCullough
    The Thorn Birds is a sweeping love story set on Drogheda, a sheep station in the Australian Outback. ... Forced to choose between the woman he loves, and the Church he is sworn to, Father Ralph's ambitions win, and he stays with the Church, eventually becoming a Cardinal in Rome
  • The Farseer Trilogy - Robin Hobb
    The Farseer Trilogy is a series of fantasy books. It follows the life of FitzChivalry Farseer (Fitz), a trained assassin, in a kingdom called The Six Duchies while his uncle, Prince Verity, attempts to wage war on the Red-Ship Raiders from The OutIslands who are attacking the shores of the kingdom by turning the people of the Six Duchies into Forged ones; a form of zombification which makes them emotionless. Meanwhile, Prince Regal's jealousy and the indulgence of his own selfish whims threatens to destroy the Six Duchies
